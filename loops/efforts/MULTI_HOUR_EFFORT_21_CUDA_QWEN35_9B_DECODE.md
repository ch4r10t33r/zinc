## Why this effort exists

Decode is the per-token autoregressive path and the one ZINC is historically *good* at: on RDNA (Effort 17) ZINC decode **beat** llama.cpp on every scenario (95вЂ“97 vs 85 tok/s). So once the CUDA kernels are numerically correct, decode parity-then-win is the **more reachable** of the two hot paths. The decode perf unlock вЂ” an async stream/event command ring that overlaps GPU exec with CPU command-building вЂ” already paid off on Metal/RDNA (+22% on the 2b SSM decode) and ports directly to `CUstream` + `CUevent`.
